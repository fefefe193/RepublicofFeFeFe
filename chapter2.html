Chapter 2: Powers and Responsibilities of the Government

Article 11:

The government is responsible for accepting citizen proposals and conducting votes.

Article 12:

The government must respect citizen opinions and make fair judgments.

Article 13:

The government is responsible for properly applying national rules and taking appropriate actions against violators.

Article 14:

The government must protect citizens' personal information and prohibit unauthorized use.

Article 15:

The government must ensure equality among citizens and prevent discriminatory actions.

Article 16:

The government is responsible for securing national safety and preventing cyber-attacks on other communities.

Article 17:

The government must reflect citizen opinions and carry out rule amendments.
