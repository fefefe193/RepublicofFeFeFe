Chapter 1: Duties of Citizens

Article 1:

Citizens are obligated to respect and engage in friendly relations with one another. Communities are regarded as nations, and members of these communities (including Community founders) are considered citizens.

Article 2:

Prohibition of topics related to elections and politics outside of the country. Offenders will face a one-day BAN after two warnings.

Article 3:

Spam activities are prohibited. Sending unrelated messages or advertisements is banned.

Article 4:

All citizens must respect people from other nations and refrain from discriminatory behavior.

Article 5:

Regardless of gender, age, nationality, or other attributes, all citizens have the right to be treated equally.

Article 6:

Rules of the nation or proposals must be detailed and decided through voting. If, one week after a proposal, the number of supporters exceeds the number of opponents, a three-day trial period will be established, after which a final vote will decide whether to implement it. All actions are based on Tokyo time.

Article 7:

Voting can be freely used not only for rule decisions but also for topics such as games and anime.

Article 8:

The transmission of images rated R18 and above is strictly prohibited. If mistakenly sent, they must be deleted immediately.

Article 9:

It is forbidden to transmit other people's personal information without permission.

Article 10:

Insulting other nations or individuals is prohibited. Mutual respect is a duty.
