Chapter 3: War and Security

Article 18: The nation renounces resolving disputes with other communities through armed force.

Article 19: The nation will not engage in cyber-attacks on other communities.

Article 20: The nation prioritizes the safety of its citizens and aims for peaceful resolutions.

Article 21: The nation shall maintain a national military and is responsible for national defense.

Article 22: The nation shall maintain an IT military and is responsible for ensuring cybersecurity.
